About Us

TechCircle exists to supercharge the African techosystem via the design, facilitation and execution of various programs for startups, founders, investors, developers and everyone in-between. We also provide advisory services for early-stage founders and investors in the areas of product development, fundraising, deal sourcing and general advisory services. We’ve been fortunate to work with local and international organizations including but not limited to Techcrunch, Paystack, Google, Facebook, Y Combinator and Microsoft.
